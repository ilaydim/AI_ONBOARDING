# TechNova — Company Overview

TechNova is a technology company founded in 2015 that builds enterprise software solutions, with a growing team of 200+ people.

## Vision
To make enterprise software human-centered, accessible, and scalable.

## Mission
To transform our customers' business processes through software — delivering this sustainably through rapid delivery, a strong engineering culture, and continuous learning.

## Products
- **TechNova Suite**: Enterprise ERP + CRM platform
- **Nova Analytics**: Real-time business intelligence
- **Nova API Gateway**: Enterprise API management platform

## Offices
- Istanbul (Headquarters)
- Ankara (R&D Center)
- Berlin (European Operations)

## Company Size & Growth
- 200+ employees across 3 offices
- Founded 2015, profitable since 2018
- Serving 150+ enterprise customers in 12 countries

2. **Engineering Excellence** — We build things right, not just fast
3. **Continuous Learning** — We invest in people growing every day
4. **Transparency** — Open communication at every level
5. **Ownership** — We take responsibility end-to-end
