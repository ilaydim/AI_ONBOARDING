# Product Management Team

The team responsible for defining what TechNova builds and why — ensuring products create real value for customers.

## Team Structure
- 4 Product Managers (1 junior/APM, 2 mid, 1 senior/Group PM)
- 1 Head of Product
- Works closely with: UX Designers, Engineering Managers, Data Analysts

## Responsibilities
- Product strategy and roadmap definition
- Customer discovery and research
- Requirements definition (PRDs, user stories)
- Prioritization and backlog management
- Cross-functional alignment (engineering, design, sales, support)
- Success metrics definition and tracking

## Product Areas

| Product | PM Owner | Description |
|---|---|---|
| TechNova Suite Core | Senior PM | ERP + CRM platform features |
| Nova Analytics | Mid PM | BI and reporting features |
| Nova API Gateway | Mid PM | API management portal |
| Platform & Growth | APM | Onboarding, billing, notifications |

## How Product Works with Engineering
1. **Discovery** (2 weeks): PM + Designer validate problem with customer research
2. **Definition** (1 week): PRD written, reviewed by engineering
3. **Sprint Planning**: PM prioritizes backlog, engineering estimates
4. **Development**: PM available for questions, reviews progress demos
5. **Launch**: PM writes release notes, coordinates with sales and support
6. **Measurement**: PM tracks success metrics post-launch

## Key Artifacts
- **PRD (Product Requirements Document)**: Defines what to build and why
- **User Stories**: `As a [role], I want [feature] so that [benefit]`
- **Roadmap**: Quarterly, reviewed monthly (in Confluence)
- **Success Metrics**: OKRs tied to each major initiative

## Decision Framework
- **Prioritization**: RICE scoring (Reach × Impact × Confidence ÷ Effort)
- **Bets vs. Bugs**: Balance new features vs. reliability improvements
- **Customer evidence required**: No major feature without at least 3 customer interviews
