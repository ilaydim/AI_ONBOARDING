# Product Management — Tools, Links & Standards

## Internal Resources
- **Confluence**: `Product` space — PRDs, roadmaps, research notes, release notes
- **Linear**: Product team tracks work in each engineering team's project
- **Figma**: Design files linked from PRDs
- **Mixpanel**: Product analytics and user behavior tracking
- **Intercom**: Customer feedback and support tickets
- **Dovetail**: User research repository

## Key Documents & Templates

### PRD Template (in Confluence)
```
1. Problem Statement
2. Customer Evidence (interviews, data)
3. Goals & Success Metrics
4. User Stories / Requirements
5. Out of Scope
6. Open Questions
7. Launch Checklist
```

### RICE Scoring
- **Reach**: How many users/month?
- **Impact**: 0.25 (minimal) → 3 (massive)
- **Confidence**: 50% → 100%
- **Effort**: Person-weeks
- Score = (Reach × Impact × Confidence) / Effort

### OKR Format
- **Objective**: Qualitative, ambitious goal
- **Key Results**: 3–5 measurable outcomes
- Reviewed monthly, updated quarterly

## Product Processes

### Customer Research
- At least 3 interviews before writing a PRD for major features
- Research notes stored in Dovetail
- Synthesis shared in weekly product review

### Release Process
1. Feature flagged off in production
2. Engineering deploys to staging
3. PM + QA validate on staging
4. PM writes release notes
5. Feature flag enabled gradually (10% → 50% → 100%)
6. PM monitors metrics for 2 weeks post-launch

### Roadmap Reviews
- **Weekly**: PM + EM sync (30 min)
- **Monthly**: Product + Engineering leadership (1 hour)
- **Quarterly**: All-hands roadmap presentation

## Key Contacts
